Use this skill when validating or presenting the strongest end-to-end demo story.

## Goal

Walk the seeded weak-signal-to-real-lead path and confirm it remains intact.

## Preferred Demo Path

Use the Mercor scenario unless the seed changes.

## Validation Checklist

1. reset demo data
2. locate the seeded social signal
3. confirm company resolution occurred
4. confirm an active listing exists
5. confirm the surfaced lead is `combined`
6. confirm the explanation and evidence mention the resolution story
7. confirm any queued recheck state still exists for unresolved signals elsewhere

## Pass Criteria

- one compelling combined lead exists
- it clearly tells the signal-to-lead story
